Project Summary: E-commerce Customer Behavior Analysis
Project Overview This project analyzes e-commerce consumer data to identify high-value segments and behavioral patterns to optimize marketing and logistics.

Key Data Insights
Top Spenders by Education: Customers with a Bachelor's degree lead spending with an average of $283.83.

Top Spenders by Age: GEN-Z is the most profitable age group, averaging $282.13 per transaction.

Top Spenders by Status: Married individuals represent the highest-spending marital category at $280.56.

Operational Efficiency: The business maintains an average delivery time of 4.2 days.

Purchase Intent: High-income customers with "Planned" intent show the highest potential spend, reaching up to $350.


Recommendations
Targeting: Focus premium advertisements on GEN-Z and Bachelor's degree holders to maximize ROI.

Engagement: Increase investment in social media marketing due to the positive correlation between platform time and total spend.

Logistics: Maintain or reduce the 4.2-day average delivery time to ensure customer satisfaction remains high.
